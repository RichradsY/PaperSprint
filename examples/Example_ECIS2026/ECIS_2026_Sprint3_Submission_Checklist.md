# ECIS 2026 Sprint 3 Submission Checklist

Updated: 2026-03-10 23:40:00 CET

## Official ECIS 2026 Template And Submission Checks

- Submission category shows exactly `TREO Paper`.
- The working file is based on the revised ECIS template dated `05 August 2025`.
- The title page is anonymous.
- No author names appear in the body, header, or footer.
- Word and PDF metadata are scrubbed before final upload.
- The abstract is `150 words or fewer`.
- The paper body starts on the first page, immediately after abstract and keywords.
- Keywords are `5 or fewer`.
- The full paper, including title, abstract, body, references, figures, tables, and appendices, stays within the `3-page` TREO limit.
- The submission is prepared in both `WORD` and `PDF` formats.
- References follow `APA 7th` style.
- The draft is uploaded through `PCS` before `2026-03-25 23:59 CET`.

## AI And Process Checks

- Check whether PCS asks for generative AI assistance disclosure at submission time.
- If disclosure is requested, describe use consistently with the actual drafting process.
- Verify that no hidden comments, tracked changes, or personal properties remain in the Word file.

## Remaining Human Checks

- Visually verify the final Word file page count after template transfer.
- Decide whether to opt into a roundtable-style TREO presentation if the submission flow offers it.
- Confirm that the final wording still matches the target track before upload.
